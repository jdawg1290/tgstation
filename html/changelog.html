05 August 2020

Timberpoes updated:

  • When hacking wires, all interfaces will now show a notice box at the top telling you which wire configuration you're attempting to hack.

04 August 2020

Krysonism updated:

  • Syringe & dart projectiles now handle reagent transfer effects as intended.
  • Also fixes reagent transfers from various other sources such as sleepy pens, cryo tubes and drink bottles.
  • As a result of the fix; Standard sized syriniver filled syringe projectiles no longer overdose the victim in one hit, unless they already have poor liver health.

Paxilmaniac updated:

  • water vapor canister to delta's janitor closet
  • a single very fancy yellow square decal under said water vapor canister

ShizCalev updated:

  • Fixed a runtime caused by trying to wash carbons who weren't holding anything.

carshalash updated:

  • Rubberneck now includes shock immunity due to containing grey bull.

03 August 2020

ATHATH updated:

  • The ammo boxes in the comms agent base now use the same ammo type as the magazines that are literally in the same crate as them. In other words, they're compatible with stetchkins now.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Getting your paycheck will no longer cause vending machines and other machines to mass-open the UI.
  • Bounty Pads no longer spontaneously combust when sending bonfires.
  • The armored bounty hunter now spawns with a backpack like the rest of them.
  • Hook Bounter Hunters now correctly spawn their outfit.
  • Plasma Tank Suit sprites.

Krysonism updated:

  • A new heretic node to replace rust wave: entropic plume.
  • A small rune visual effects appears 70% of the time when a tile is rusted.
  • Removed the rust wave node.
  • The ash man and rust walker nodes now link to the correct nodes.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Lots of mob emotes will actually work now. Observe your local Runtime today

LemonInTheDark, Stylemistake updated:

  • Added an atmos subsystem control panel to the debug menu, don't press buttons if you don't want to freeze atmos/show a bunch of colored tiles. It shows excited groups, not active turfs. Fair warning, displaying shit will cause a mite slowdown, so don't just leave it on in the background all the time.

ShizCalev updated:

  • Fixed the Saber SMG and Tommygun's empty chamber indicators not appearing.
  • Added support to pixel offset suppressors, allowing you to have ones that extend outside of the gun's 32x32 icon.
  • Removed a number of unused overlays being added to ballistic guns.
  • The C20r's empty indicator is no longer covered up by the fire select indicator.
  • Fixed admin deleted suppressors not properly updating a gun's weight.
  • Varediting a ballistic weapon's vars which have icons associated with them will now make the gun update it's icons.
  • Setting your firemode to burst will now say "3-round burst" instead of "3-rnd burst".
  • C20r's description now correctly says 3 round burst instead of 2 round burst.
  • Ballistic weapons with non-open bolts will now correctly spawn with a full magazine.
  • Hooked up delta's perma APC to the power grid. honk.
  • Fixed a runtime caused by singularities pulling turf without plating.
  • Singularities will now strip away mineral plated turf.
  • Fixed singularities creating new tiles for turf that had destroyed plating.
  • Made the notification for the malf AI overload ability a little less vague about what was destroyed.
  • Pets will no longer breed with their children.
  • Pets will no longer breed infinitely despite having a ton of children around.

SomeguyManperson updated:

  • hypersensitive and apathetic apply their modifiers after moodlets are accounted for rather than multiplying total mood per moodlet
